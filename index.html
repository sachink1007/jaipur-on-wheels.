About Our Royal Journey

Experience Jaipur like never before aboard our luxury dining bus. Our custom-designed vehicle accommodates 20 guests in premium comfort, featuring a fully equipped kitchen and exquisite decor that captures the essence of Rajasthani royalty.

As you savor our carefully crafted fusion cuisine, watch the Pink City unfold through our panoramic glass walls and roof, creating memories that will last a lifetime.

✨ Premium Features

  • 🪵 Handcrafted wooden interiors
  • 💡 Ambient LED lighting system
  • 🔍 250° transparent glass walls & roof
  • 🏰 Heritage-inspired luxury decor
  • 👥 Intimate seating for 20 guests
  • 🍴 On-board gourmet kitchen
